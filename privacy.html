Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

This policy is written for Packet Cards and packetcards.com. It is not legal advice; have a qualified lawyer review it before relying on it for regulated or large-scale business use.

Who We Are

Packet Cards operates packetcards.com, a website for digital business cards, card setup, profile viewing, business card previews, and business inquiries. You can contact us at joseandrescruz.jac@gmail.com.

Age Requirement

Packet is not directed to children under 13. Do not create an account, submit an inquiry, or provide personal information if you are under 13. If we learn that we collected personal information from a child under 13 without appropriate parental consent, we will delete it. The FTC explains that COPPA gives parents control over information websites collect from children under 13.

Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to provide, including:

  • Account information, such as email address and password authentication handled through Firebase.
  • Digital card profile details, such as name, title, company, bio, location, websites, social links, work links, credentials, phone number, WhatsApp, Telegram, and profile or logo images you upload.
  • Business inquiry details, such as your name, company, work email, phone number, and message.
  • Technical information that may be processed by hosting, Firebase, browser, or form services, such as IP address, device, browser, timestamps, and security logs.

How We Use Information

We use information to create and display Packet card profiles, let card owners manage their cards, respond to business inquiries, improve the website, prevent abuse, protect security, and comply with applicable laws.

Public Profile Information

Information placed on a Packet profile may be visible to anyone with the card link or QR/NFC destination. Only add information you are comfortable sharing publicly.

Service Providers

We may use service providers to operate the website, including GitHub Pages for hosting, Firebase for authentication and database services, and FormSubmit or a similar form processor for business inquiry emails. These providers process information as needed to provide their services.

Cookies and Analytics

Packet does not intentionally use advertising cookies. Some service providers may use essential storage, cookies, or logs to keep the site secure and functional. If analytics are added later, this policy should be updated before use.

Data Retention

We keep account, card, and inquiry information as long as needed to provide Packet services, respond to requests, maintain business records, or comply with legal obligations. You may ask us to delete your information using the contact email above.

Your Choices

You can edit your card information through the card management page. You may request access, correction, or deletion by emailing us. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards and rely on reputable service providers. No website or internet transmission is perfectly secure, so do not submit sensitive information that should not appear on a business card or inquiry email.

International Users

Packet is operated from the Philippines and may use service providers in other countries. By using the site, you understand that information may be processed where our providers operate.

Changes

We may update this policy as Packet changes. The effective date above shows when this version took effect.
